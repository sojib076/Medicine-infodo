## Indications
Treatment of dandruff & seborrhoeic dermatitis.

## Description
- lastingly cleanses
- eliminates persistent dandruff and prevents their recurrence
- instantly soothes fluid creamy texture - fresh and light fragrance

## Dosage & Administration
- As intense treatment (3 weeks): 3 shampoos per week
- Maintenance: 1 to 2 shampoos per week
- Lather node ds+ shampoo by gently massaging the scalp.
- Rinse and repeat the application. Leave on for 5 minutes, then rinse thoroughly.

## Storage Conditions
Keep below 30°C temperature, away from light & moisture. Keep out of the reach of children.
