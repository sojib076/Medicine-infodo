## Indications
Relief of symptoms associated with hemorrhoids, Proctitis, and perianal irritation. Provides soothing, anti-inflammatory, and moisturizing effects.

## Composition
Procto Cream contains: 

- Olea Europaea Fruit Oil,
- Cera Alba,
- Pistacia Lentiscus,
- Hypericum Perforatum Extract,
- Calendula Officinalis Flower Extract,
- Salvia Officinalis,
- Tocopherol Extract.

## Dosage & Administration
- Apply externally to the anal and perianal region 2-3 times daily, or as directed by a physician.
- Cleanse the area thoroughly before application.
- For external use only.

## Contraindications
Known hypersensitivity to ingredients

## Side Effects
Generally, well tolerated.

## Pregnancy & Lactation
Safe for use during pregnancy and lactation when applied externally as directed. No systemic absorption or adverse maternal/fetal effects reported.

## Therapeutic Class
Non-steroidal Anti-inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs)

## Storage Conditions
Store below 25°C, protected from light and moisture. Keep out of reach of children.
