## Indications
Sac Sweet is used as a sweetening agent. It is an artificial sweetener with effectively no food energy. It is about 300-400 times as sweet as sucrose but has a bitter or metallic aftertaste, especially at high concentrations. Sac Sweet is used to sweeten products such as drinks, candies, cookies, and medicines.

## Dosage & Administration
As per advised by a the physician. 1 tab is equivalent in sweetners to 1 tespoon full of sugar.

## Interaction
There are no known drug interactions and none well documented.

## Side Effects
Sac Sweet belongs to a class of compounds known as sulfonamides, which can cause allergic reactions in some individuals. Reactions can include: Headaches Breathing difficulties Diarrhoea and Skin problems

## Pregnancy & Lactation
Saccharin Sodium is safe in pregnancy & lactation.

## Precautions & Warnings
Possibility of allergic reactions.

## Therapeutic Class
Oral nutritional preparations

## Storage Conditions
Keep below 30°C temperature, away from light & moisture. Keep out of the reach of children.
