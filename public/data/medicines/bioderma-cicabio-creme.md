## Indications
Bioderma Cicabio Cream+: treatment cream for soothong & reparing of non-oozing/ dry skin lesion.

## Description
- Deep moisturisation
- Hyaluronic acid
- Surface moisturisation
- Polyglutamic acid
- Restructuring agent (collagen booste): Xylose
- Glycerin & hyaluronic acid: moisturizes and protect

## Dosage & Administration
Every day, 2-3 times 
Apply onto affected area. Use until completely healed.

## Storage Conditions
Keep below 30°C temperature, away from light & moisture. Keep out of the reach of children.
