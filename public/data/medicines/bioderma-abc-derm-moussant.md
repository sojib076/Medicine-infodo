## Indications
ABCDerm Moussant : Regular foaming cleanse for normal baby & children's skin. 

ABCDerm Hydratant : Regular moisturizer for normal baby & children’s skin.

## Description
ABCDerm Moussant- 

- Gently cleanse: Non-stripping cleansing base, ultra-mild surfactants
- Sodium laureth sulfate: Mild surfactant
- Sodium citrate: Balance the ph levels of body
ABCDerm Hydratant- 

- Skin barrier therapytm complex: limits the adhesion of factors that responsible for skin irritation.
- lipigeniumtm complex: creates a natural protective film and durably nourishes the skin.
- glycerine + vaseline: moisturise, soften and protect

## Dosage & Administration
ABCDerm Moussant: Apply once or twice a day on wet skin and hair. Work into a foam. Rinse well. Gently dry. 

ABCDerm Hydratant: Apply thoroughly once or twice a day on dried skin after cleansing with a cleanser (abcderm moussant). Gently massage until absorbed.

## Storage Conditions
Keep below 30°C temperature, away from light & moisture.
