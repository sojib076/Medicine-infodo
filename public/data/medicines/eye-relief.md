## Indications
Eye Relief removes loose foreign material, air pollutants (smog or pollen), chlorinated water for washing the eye to relieve- 

- irritation,
- stinging,
- discomfort,
- itching,
- burning

## Composition
Active Ingredient : Purified Water INN (99.05%) 
Inactive Ingredients : Boric Acid, Borax, Sodium Chloride 
Preservative : Disodium Edetate and Sorbic Acid

## Pharmacology
Eye Relief, eye irrigating solution for eye wash can be used for daily or emergency eye cleansing. Boric Acid has mild antibiotic properties against fungal or bacterial infection. Boric Acid is used in eye wash to cleanse or irrigate the eyes. Boric Acid provides soothing relief from eye irritation, and helps remove pollutants from the eye such as smog, chlorine, or other chemicals

## Dosage & Administration
Instill in the affected eyes as needed or prescribed by the physician. 

Using without Eye Cup 

- Flush the affected eye(s) as needed
- Control the rate of flow of solution by pressure on the bottle
- Do not touch dropper tip to eye
Using with Eye Cup 

- Fill the cup upto the 5 ml round mark with Eye Relief eye wash solution and apply the cup to the affected eye(s), pressing tightly to prevent leakage
- Tilt the head backward. Open eyelids wide and rotate eyeball to wash the eye throughly
- Rinse cup with clean water after each use
- Close the cap after use

## Contraindications
No data available.

## Pregnancy & Lactation
Safe use during pregnancy and lactation has not been established. If pregnant or breast-feeding, ask your doctor before use.

## Precautions & Warnings
- Do not use if you have open wounds in or near the eyes, and get medical help right away
- Do not use if solution changes color or becomes cloudy
- Remove contact lenses before using
- Do not touch tip of container to any surface to avoid contamination
- Replace cap after use
- Stop use and ask a doctor if you experience eye pain, changes in vision, continued redness or irritation of the eye
- Condition worsens or persists
- If swallowed, get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away.

## Therapeutic Class
Other ophthalmic preparations

## Storage Conditions
Store at 15°C to 30°C in a dry place protected from light. It is desirable that the contents should not be used more than one month after first opening of the bottle. Keep out of reach of children.
