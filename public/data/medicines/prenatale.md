## Indications
Prevention and reduction of stretch marks by keeping the skin hydrated and resilient during pregnancy, adolescence, or rapid weight changes.

## Composition
Prenatale Gel contains: 

- Triticum Vulgare Bran Extract,
- Salvia Officinalis Extract,
- Allantoin,
- Calendula Officinalis Flower Extract,
- Olea Europaea Leaf Extract,
- Chamomilla Recutita Flower Extract

## Dosage & Administration
Apply to clean and dry skin area (abdomen, thighs, hips, and breasts) two to three times a day. Massage gently for 5 minutes until absorbed.

## Contraindications
- Hypersensitivity to ingredients
- Do not apply on broken or infected skin

## Side Effects
Generally, well tolerated. Rarely, mild local irritation (redness, itching, burning). Discontinue if persistent.

## Pregnancy & Lactation
Safe for use during pregnancy and breastfeeding. No reported adverse effects when used as directed.

## Therapeutic Class
Miscellaneous topical agents
