## Indications
Moxacil is indicated for the treatment of infections caused by susceptible bacteria: Upper and lower respiratory tract infections, Urinary tract infections (cystitis, pyelonephritis), Skin and soft tissue infections, Otitis media, Sinusitis, Dental abscesses, H. pylori eradication therapy.

## Composition
Each capsule contains: Amoxicillin Trihydrate BP equivalent to Amoxicillin 500 mg. Excipients: Magnesium Stearate, Colloidal Silicon Dioxide, Gelatin (capsule).

## Pharmacology
Same mechanism as Amoxicillin 250 mg (aminopenicillin, cell wall synthesis inhibitor). The 500 mg dose provides higher serum and tissue levels suitable for moderate to severe infections. Bioavailability is approximately 75-90% and is not significantly affected by food intake.

## Dosage & Administration
Adults:
- Mild to moderate infections: 500 mg every 8 hours.
- Severe infections: 500 mg every 6 hours or 875 mg every 12 hours.
- H. pylori eradication: 1 g twice daily for 7 days with PPI and clarithromycin.
- Dental infections: 500 mg every 8 hours for 5 days.

Children over 40 kg: same as adult dose. Children under 40 kg: 45 mg/kg/day in three divided doses.

## Interaction
Probenecid increases amoxicillin serum levels. Enhanced effect of oral anticoagulants. Reduced absorption with tetracyclines. Possible increase in methotrexate toxicity.

## Contraindications
Hypersensitivity to amoxicillin, penicillins, or any excipient. History of severe immediate hypersensitivity reaction to beta-lactam antibiotics.

## Side Effects
Common: diarrhoea, nausea, rash. Uncommon: vomiting. Rare: anaphylaxis, pseudomembranous colitis, hepatocellular toxicity, interstitial nephritis.

## Pregnancy & Lactation
Generally considered safe in pregnancy. Small amounts pass into breast milk; rarely causes diarrhoea, candidiasis, or sensitisation in infants.

## Precautions & Warnings
Culture and sensitivity testing recommended. Monitor for superinfection in prolonged use. Check for penicillin hypersensitivity. Reduce dose in renal impairment.

## Overdose Effects
Similar to 250 mg. Crystalluria possible in high doses with inadequate fluid intake. Treat symptomatically.

## Therapeutic Class
Aminopenicillin antibiotic

## Storage Conditions
Store below 25°C in a dry place, protected from moisture. Keep out of reach of children.
