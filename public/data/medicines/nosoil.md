## Indications
Nosoil is indicated in- 

- Dry & Crusty Nose,
- Rhinitis,
- Itching or burning feeling of nose,
- During/after the common cold,
- Oxygen therapy,
- After nasal surgery,
- Nose bleeding

## Dosage & Administration
Adults : 2-4 drops or sprays per nostril 2-3 times daily. 
Children from 4 to 12 years : 1-2 drops or sprays per nostril twice daily. 

To be dispensed only by or on the prescription of a registered physician.

## Pregnancy & Lactation
Safe during pregnancy and lactation.

## Therapeutic Class
Herbal and Nutraceuticals

## Storage Conditions
Keep below 30°C temperature, away from light & moisture. Keep out of the reach of children.
