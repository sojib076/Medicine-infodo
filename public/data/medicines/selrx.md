## Side Effects
SelRx Shampoo with Salicylic Acid is usually well accepted & tolerated. As with other shampoos, a burning sensation, itching or skin irritations which is very rare, may occur on exposed areas. Seek medical attention right away if any of these Severe side effects occurs. Severe allergic reactions (rash; hives; itching; difficulty breathing; tightness in the chest; swelling of the mouth, face, lips, or tongue); severe skin irritation.

## Pregnancy & Lactation
SelRx Shampoo can be used during Pregnancy and Breast Feeding. If you face any complicacy or have query, please ask your physician.

## Storage Conditions
It should be kept in a safe place where children cannot reach. Store at or below 30°celsius.
