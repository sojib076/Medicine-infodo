## Dosage & Administration
Sensibio Eye Contour Gel : Apply sensibio eye 1 to 2 times per day to eye contour already washed with sensibio h2o micellar solution. Gently massage so that it penetrates the skin. 

Sensibio DS+ Cream : Apply sensibio ds+ creme 1 to 2 times per day on skin already washed with sensibio ds+ cleansing gel. Gently massage until absorbed. 

Sensibio H2O : Soak a cotton pad with sensibio H2O and then cleanse and/or remove make-up from the face and eyes. Repeat application until the cotton pad is perfectly clean. Gently pat the skin to dry it. No rinsing is required.

## Storage Conditions
Keep below 30°C temperature, away from light & moisture. Keep out of the reach of children.
