## Dosage & Administration
How to use Bioderma Sebium Gel Moussant Actif : 

- Apply morning and/or evening to damp skin.
- Massage gently to create a foamy lather.
- Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.
- Avoid contact with eyes. Discontinue use if irritation occurs and consult a dermatologist.
How to use Bioderma Sébium Kerato+ : 

- Cleanse skin with Sébium Gel Moussant Actif or Sébium Gel Moussant
- Apply Sébium Kerato+ morning and/or evening to the entire face or on targeted problem areas
- Gently massage until fully absorbed
- Use at the first signs of spot formation for best results
How to use Sébium Pore Refiner : 

- helps to visibly minimize the look of pores and improve skin texture appearance.
- Silica powder and anti-reflection particules ensure a powdery and mattifying effect. Salicylic acid exfoliates to enhance radiance.
How to use Sebium Hydra : 

- Everyday morning and/ evening
- Apply on skin after cleansing with sebium bar/ sebium foaming gel.
