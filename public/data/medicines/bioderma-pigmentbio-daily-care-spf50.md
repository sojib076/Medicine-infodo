## Dosage & Administration
Pigmentbio Foaming cream 

- Everyday morning and/ evening
- Apply on wet skin, lather, rinse and dry gently
- Can be used as a face mask: apply a thick layer for 5 minutes, moisten, lather and rinse thoroughly
Pigmentbio Night renewer 

- Everyday evening
- Apply on skin (face & neck) after cleansing with pigmentbio foaming cream
- Can be used alone or after pigmentbio c contentrate
Pigmentbio C-Concentrate 

- Everyday evening
- Apply 5 drops on skin (face & neck) after cleansing with pigmentbio foaming cream
- Better result is the association with pigmentbio daily care spf 50+
Pigmentbio Daily care SPF 50+ 

- Everyday morning
- Apply on skin (face & neck) after cleansing with pigmentbio foaming cream
Pigmentbio Sensitive areas 

- Everyday morning and/ evening
- Apply to affected areas after cleansing with pigmentbio foaming cream

## Storage Conditions
Keep below 30°C temperature, away from light & moisture. Keep out of the reach of children.
