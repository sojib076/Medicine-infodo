## Indications
Some of the benefits of Monimix Plus are given below: 

- Helps prevent anaemia and micronutrient deficiencies in children (5-12 years of age)
- Improves body's immune system
- Increases child's appetite and overall nutritional status
- Boost child's ability to learn and develops productivity
- Reduces risk of infections

## Description
Monimix Plus is a micronutrient powder having MoniMix Plus recommended by WHO which helps prevent anemia and micronutrient deficiencies in children from 5 to 12 years of age. SMC introduced Monimix Plus in April, 2021 as a public health product aiming to address the acute malnutrition among the children in Bangladesh.

## Dosage & Administration
A child should be fed a total of 180 sachets per year taking one sachet daily for three consecutive months with three months' interval. MoniMix Plus will improve the nutritional status of children, make them more productive and thereby contribute to the overall development of the country.
